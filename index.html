The eDreams.io

A smart object able to collecte datas about your sleep, via many sensors.

Everything you know and and you want to know about the sleep troubles, all with dashboard widgets. Fully accessible with PCs and smart phones .

Easily control your eDreams via a simple mobile application. No need to be an expert. You can simply use the app to monitor your sleep quality.

Made to be Easy

Versatile and unique. The eDreams is breadboard is Arduino-compatible, tiny enough to fit in most enclosures, and compatible with standard Arduino shields . For more details on the shield assembly or the eDreams, visit our GitHube page.

Just one app

A single app with multiple applications. Rather than having a ton of examples fractured across different apps, we have all of our examples within one simple and elegant app. Each example, or as we call it, module, is fully expandable and powered by the eDreams iOS library. You can build apps based on our modules or scrap the whole thing and just use our eDreams library.

Completely open

Built with the community in mind, eDreams.io is as open as it gets.

All of our Apps, including the iPhone app, are free and open source. We can't wait to see what inspire you!

Our schematics, board design, bill of materials, and firmware, are all open source. We love open hardware and hope you do too.

The eDreams is 100% javascript funded. We had permanently in mind the modern way of web architectures.

Tech specs

eDreams station

  • Arduino UNO
  • DHT22 sensor
  • MAX9135 Audio sensor
  • TSL2651 Light sensor
  • Onboard 5V Regulator
  • BtBee Pro bluetooth modem
  • Serial Communication:
    UART, SPI, I2C
  • CC3000 wifi module
  • LEDs for serial communication

eDreams watch

  • Arduino Pro Mini
  • 16 MHz Clock Speed
  • 32 KB Flash Memory
    4KB used by bootloader
  • 2.5 KB SRAM
  • 1 KB EPROM
  • BtBee Pro bluetooth modem

Software Specs

  • NodeJS
  • Mongo DB
  • Angular JS
  • Express
  • Socket IO
